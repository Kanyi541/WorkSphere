Our Founder’s Story

Meet Emmah Wanjiru Ng'ang'a, the Visionary Behind WorkSphere Solutions

With a strong background in strategic business development and a passion for authentic growth, Emmah Wanjiru Ng'ang'a founded WorkSphere Solutions to revolutionize how consultancy is approached in Africa. She identified a critical gap in the industry—one lacking identity-focused, emotionally intelligent strategies that support meaningful, sustainable business growth.

Her journey began after witnessing how countless small businesses in Kenya had tremendous potential, but lacked the systems and strategic frameworks needed to scale while preserving their unique identity.

Today, she leads a dynamic team committed to transforming African enterprises through intentional, ecosystem-driven growth strategies—not just quick fixes. At WorkSphere, we believe in building from within, nurturing both vision and values.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with startups, SMEs, non-profits, and government organizations across sectors such as agriculture, finance, education, health, and technology. Our solutions are tailored to each client's industry and goals.

What services does your consultancy offer?

Our core services include project management, business development, digital transformation, cloud solutions, data analytics, grant proposal writing, and economic policy advisory.

How do you price your services?

Pricing depends on the scope, duration, and complexity of the project. We offer both fixed-rate packages and flexible hourly or retainer models. Custom quotes are provided after a free initial consultation.

How do I start working with your team?

Simply contact us through our website or email to schedule a discovery call. During this session, we’ll understand your needs, propose a strategy, and define the next steps toward execution.

Is my company’s data and information secure?

Absolutely. We adhere to industry best practices in data protection, including confidentiality agreements, secure cloud storage, and GDPR compliance where applicable.

What is the typical project duration?

Project timelines vary based on the complexity and deliverables involved. Some projects take 2–4 weeks, while strategic initiatives may run for several months. Timelines are clearly defined during onboarding.

Still have questions?

We’re here to help. Reach out to us directly and one of our consultants will be happy to provide answers tailored to your specific situation.
